Pizza Trivia

When did Pizza First appear in India

Pizza first appeared in India in the early 1900s, introduced by Italian missionaries. However, it wasn't until the late 1990s that pizza gained widespread popularity, primarily due to the arrival of international pizza chains like Domino's Pizza and Pizza Hut. These chains helped to introduce the concept of pizza to a wider Indian audience and made it a popular fast-food option.
